Projects

* Speak, Sound, Persuade: The Role Of Voice Identity in Propaganda Awareness *(Master’s Thesis, Apr. 2024 – Oct. 2025)*  
  * Designed and developed **CritiClone**, an AI-driven system integrating propaganda detection and voice-based interaction.  
  * Built a fully deployable full-stack application with FastAPI, Next.js, and DynamoDB, containerized via Docker Compose under a privacy-first design.
